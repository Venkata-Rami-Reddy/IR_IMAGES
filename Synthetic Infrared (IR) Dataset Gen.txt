Synthetic Infrared (IR) Dataset Generation

This project generates a small synthetic infrared (IR) image dataset with realistic thermal and sensor-like characteristics. The goal is to simulate a cooler background, warmer moving objects, and temporal variation across multiple frames, along with IR sensor effects such as noise and blur.

ðŸ“œ Technical Summary

The dataset uses simple thermal modeling to represent emissivity-like behavior. Noise, blur, and 8-bit intensity scaling simulate a low-resolution thermal sensor. Limitations include lack of radiometric modeling, emissivity physics, or environmental temperature variation. Future improvements could add multiple objects, thermal decay curves, dead pixels, and realistic background textures.
